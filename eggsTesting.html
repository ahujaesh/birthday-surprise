Goal: Find all the dragon eggs!

Eggs found: 0




Instructions: Now that you have completed the tutorial page (If you haven't go here), you can be loaded into the actual world. In this game, a mother dragon has lost all of her dragon eggs, and it is up to you to find all seven of them. The eggs are suposed to be found in order of their colors. For example, you should go and find the red egg first, press the "I found an egg" button, then go to the orange egg, press the "I found an egg" button, go to the yellow egg, ect. The controls are the same as the tutorial. and once you find the purple egg, you will recieve instructions on how to proceed. Good luck and have fun!
